## Description

To improve its AI capabilities, SkySoft has recently upgraded SkyBot by integrating a new large language model: Qwen2.5-1.5B-Instruct. The update promised faster responses, better reasoning, and a more natural conversational experience, making SkyBot even more valuable for both customers and internal staff.

Shortly after the upgrade, however, users began reporting strange behavior. During certain conversations, SkyBot becomes unusually defensive, refusing to answer specific requests and acting as if it's protecting confidential information hidden within its instructions.
